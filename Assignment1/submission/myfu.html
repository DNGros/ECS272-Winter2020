Visualizing with t-SNE - Mingye Fu

Image source: Visualizing with t-SNE

What is being shown? The above picture shows an example of t-SNE visualization. It is one way to tackle high dimensional visualization problems.

Is the visualization interactive or static? The t-SNE visualization is static.

What, in your opinion, is the rationale behind the design choices? Instead of trying to maintain the global structure, as what was implemented by many other dimensionality reduction methods, t-SNE tries to group local data points closer to each other.

Do you think this is a good visualization? I think it's a good visualization method, grouping local data points closer to each other is a better match for building human reasoning.
