Image source: Post on Reddit

This static visualization describes reccomended types of food to pair with different styles of wine. A pairing is denoted by a line drawn from the food to the wine. The color of the line is also an indicator of what style of wine the paring is for. This allows the viewer to quickly figure out which foods go which wines by viewing the color underneath the food group, or visa versa by scanning horizontally to see which colors are under which food groups. I enjoy this picture because of the artist's use of color to quickly convey information on a topic in either direction.
