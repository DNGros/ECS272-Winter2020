ECS 272 InfoVis Assignment 1

Image source: Wind Map Homepage

Shows current and historic wind data across the US. Wind direction and speed are shown using particle animation.

The visualization is interactive. The user can zoom and pan using the mouse. Furthermore, there are tooltips on hover showing details about the location pointed at.
The color palette is gray. Wind speeds are indicated through brightness and animation speed of the particles. There is a legend showing examplary speeds and what they look like on the map.

The visualization is decent. Color could have been used more to emphasize speeds or directions.
