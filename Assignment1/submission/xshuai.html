Manhattan's Dynamic Population

Image source: Manhattan Population Explorer

This is a three-dimensional and interactive visualization showing the flow of people at different places in the Manhattan area at different times of a week. Users can choose different time and different visible districts for showing.

Users can choose different time and different visible districts for showing.It use histograms of different colors to show the population which is really obvious.
