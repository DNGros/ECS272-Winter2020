Image source: Charles Joseph Minard: Napoleon's Retreat From Moscow

This chart traces the army location and size over time during the Russian campaign.

The website is interactive. Details of different events will pop up upon hovering.
In addition, casualties and temperatures are also provided when dragging the button on the chart.

The width of the band represents the size of the army. The color indicates advance or retreat of the army.
Temperatures are also provided in a parallel line chart.

In my opinion, this is a good example of visualization. The chart successfully visualizes locations, time, population and temperatures in a concise and explicit way.
